1. Signup for https://www.noip.com/.
2. get a hostname after signing in to your account.
3. Download the No-ip desktop app from https://www.noip.com/download?page=win.
4. After successfully installing the application select the hostname that you just created.
5. After that forward a port on your router to listen for connections outside your network as well.
6. Then run the server.py with the hostname and port that you just forwaded on your router.
7. fill in the hostname in client.py along with port and then run it to connect to the server.
8. Once connected type --help on server propmt to list down the possible options that you can have.
